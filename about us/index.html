The Ielts Academy

In an increasingly unified world, language proficiency is significant for personal and professional growth. At The Ielts Academy ,we provide a comprehensive full-time Academic and General IELTS preparation course , consisting of a four week programme. The programme focuses on specific academic and general IELTS exam content and techniques.We aim to be the quintessence of academic excellence, and aspire our students to be ready for university, work or immigrate in an English-speaking country.

  • The Ielts Exam

    IELTS – the International English Language Testing System – is the world’s most popular English language test.

  • The Ielts General

    Take IELTS General Training to , work in an English speaking country or your own country and emigrate to an English speaking country.

  • The Ielts Academic

    The IELTS Academic test is for your higher education ambitions ,or if you want to work in a professional organisation in an English speaking country.
