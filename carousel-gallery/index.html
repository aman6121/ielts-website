Ielts

Ielts (international English language testing system) is the world's most popular English language test for higher education and global migration. The ielts academy can help you on your journey with Ielts.

Your ielts score

Your ielts score plays a significant role in securing a visa, admission or job in leading English-speaking economies. A high score can set you apart from other applicants and give your profile a significant edge. The ielts academy provides an intensive training program designed to help you achieve your highest score.

Importance of English

This course is designed to help students improve their performance in the ielts (international English language testing system). The focus of this 4-week programme is not only to equip students with effective examination strategies, but also to develop a wide range of academic skills, including academic writing, critical thinking, and public speaking, all of which are indispensable for success at an English-medium university.

What we bring

We use proven best-practice teaching methods, and materials that have been developed by examiners, and experts in the ielts field. Our goal is to assist you to take the ielts test with confidence and to achieve the ielts score your need. We pride ourselves in our high success rate, our excellence in teaching, and our professionalism.

Success in Ielts

Succeeding in the ielts test is all about understanding the test structure. We will help you with that too. Throughout your course you will do regular test practice and role plays. You will learn special test strategies to do with the format of the test and time management.
